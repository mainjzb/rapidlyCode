<h1 style="text-align: center;"><span style="color: #000000;"><strong><span style="font-size: 18.6667px;"><u>MAPLE EXPIDITION - MISTY ISLAND LEGACY</u></span></strong></span></h1>
<h3><span style="color: #800080;"><strong><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><a name="MIL1"></a>Misty Island Legacy Chapters and Battles</span></span></strong></span></h3>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>Chapter 1 - UTC: December 14th (after maintenance) – January 24th at 11:59 PM<br></strong><strong>Chapter 2 - UTC: December 21st at 12:00 AM – January 24th at 11:59 PM<br></strong><strong>Chapter 3 and Challenge Mode - UTC: December 28th at 12:00 AM – January 24th at 11:59 PM</strong></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>Requirement: Lv. 101 and above characters (Zero characters must have completed up to Chapter 2).</strong></span></p>
<ul>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Get ready to try and survive during the Misty Island Legacy Event!</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">To start your adventure, accept "Pollion's Message, depart to Misty Island" by talking to Pollion through the event notifier on the left side of the screen.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">After clearing the tutorial, the Misty Island Exploration Map can be accessed through Pollion in the Temporary Expedition Headquarters.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Each chapter, along with Endless Mode, can be accessed through the Misty Island Exploration map.</span>
<ul>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">The expedition can be ended early by selecting the "Return" button on the right-hand side of the screen.</span></li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Chapter completion rewards and daily experience rewards can be claimed through Pollion.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">It's best to play a chapter that suits your current trait levels repeatedly to increase your odds of surviving higher chapters.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">You will earn Exploration points based on the number of days you survived and the amount of goblin attacks defeated.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Exploration points and traits can be viewed/upgraded through Sonia. Certain skill levels only become available upon completing higher chapters.</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>Central Ruins</strong></span></p>
<ul>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">The main objective is to defend the Central Ruins.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">There is no way to repair the Central Ruins, keep it safe from invaders!</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">The current expedition will end if the Central Ruins is destroyed.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">The ruin's HP is linked with the gauge of upper UI.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">If an invasion occurs while you're in a location other than the Center of the Ancient Ruins, the Central Ruins HP will decrease periodically.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Different routes around the central ruins will open up based on the scenario chosen. The goblins will always begin their attack from the edges of the map.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Carly will construct special areas on each invasion route to place defense facilities.</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>Raid and Invasion</strong></span></p>
<ul>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Invasion begins every night on the 3rd day.</span>
<ul>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">There will be a notice before the invasion begins.</span></li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Monsters summoned by the invasion do not disappear until all monsters are defeated.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Wave per chapter</span>
<ul>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Chapter 1: 3 Waves.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Chapter 2: 4 Waves.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Chapter 3: 4 Waves.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Challenge Mode: 8 Waves.</span></li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Raids appear the night before the invasion begins.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Goblins summoned by raids disappear in the morning. Goblins that are attacking the central ruins do not and must be defeated.</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>Defense Facilities</strong></span></p>
<ul>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">There are 2 types of structures that can be crafted to defend the central ruins.</span><br>
<ul>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Defense Walls: Structures that block the movement of monsters. Some defense walls also deal damage to enemies at the cost of lower durability. Defense walls can be destroyed by monsters' attacks.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Defense Towers: Structures that attack monsters and have unique effects such as slowing enemies, creating areas that apply damage over time, or just dealing tons of damage. Towers can be destroyed by monsters' attacks.</span></li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Defense structures can be placed only in predefined areas created by Carly.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Molar Traps: A spiky defensive trap perfect for softening up incoming enemies. The trap deals damage to Monsters that walk over it, making it a great source of additional damage during the invasions!</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Sticky Platform: A sticky trap that impairs enemy movement. The trap slows down monsters that walk over it, increasing the amount of time it will take to reach the Central Ruins.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Monster Sound Speaker: A powerful speaker that demoralizes even the most powerful of foes. Enemies afflicted by the speaker will deal reduced damage.</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>Monsters</strong></span></p>
<ul>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Misty Island Wildlife</span><br>
<ul>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Your main source of acquiring various resources and materials necessary for surviving your expedition.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Rare mythical beasts with powerful crafting materials can be found scattered throughout the island.</span></li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Goblins</span><br>
<ul>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Goblins will periodically attack and attempt to destroy the central ruins.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Each goblin type has a unique attack pattern, some more deadly than others.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Goblins that are part of the raid on the outskirts of the ruins are aggressive and will chase after you given the chance.</span></li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Goblin Leaders</span><br>
<ul>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Goblin Leaders will occasionally join invasions on the central ruins. They have much higher hp and damage than the average goblin troop, so tread lightly.</span>
<ul>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Goblin King</span>
<ul>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">The king of the goblins and the wielder of the Staff of Mother Earth. With higher Hp, Damage, and the ability to call for extra goblins, be prepared for a difficult fight when he appears.</span></li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>

<h3><span style="color: #800080;"><strong><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><a name="MIL4"></a>Misty Island Legacy Character and Survival Elements</span></span></strong></span></h3>
<ul>
<li>Survival Stats
<ul>
<li>Stats that must be managed in order to survive.
<ul>
<li>Life Force</li>
<li>Acting Power</li>
<li>Fullness</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Survival stats are consistently consumed at regular intervals or during certain actions and can be recovered by eating or resting.</li>
<li>At the beginning, everything starts with the max value. When your Life Force reaches 0, the game ends.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Battle Stats
<ul>
<li>Below are the stats related to battle and survival.
<ul>
<li>Attack Power</li>
<li>Defense</li>
<li>Speed</li>
<li>Jump</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>You can improve the value by equipping equipment or by using trait skills.</li>
<li>You can temporarily affect the value through various interactions.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Atmospheric Effects
<ul>
<li>Atmospheric Effects are environmental elements that change irregularly or happen based on specific conditions.</li>
<li>Atmospheric Effects can affect your character's survival stats.</li>
<li>The effect can be weakened by certain structures, equipment, and consumables.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Atmospheric Effect Particle Settings
<ul>
<li>Particles can be turned off by adjusting "Graphics Options" as particles can cause performance issues on some low spec PCs.</li>
<li>When the background quality is set to the lowest quality, the particle effect of the Atmospheric Effect will not appear on the screen.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Landmarks
<ul>
<li>Landmarks that are placed within the map to perform special functions.
<ul>
<li>Ancient Sanctuary - provides buffs for Life Force, Acting Power, and Fullness</li>
<li>Waterfall - provides water resource</li>
<li>Ancient Ruins - gives information about relics</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Trait
<ul>
<li>Traits can be used to enhance character stats by consuming 'Exploration Points' which can be acquired through gameplay.</li>
<li>Classified into 3 categories based on the type of trait:
<ul>
<li>Battle</li>
<li>Survival</li>
<li>Crafting</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>

<h3><span style="color: #800080;"><strong><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><a name="MIL3"></a>Misty Island Legacy Crafting</span></span></strong></span></h3>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>Crafting Type</strong></span></p>
<ul>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Crafting</span><br>
<ul>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Craft tools/equipment/building and other items for survival.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Some basic items can be crafted at any time with more powerful items requiring a crafting facility</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">The list of items that can be crafted increases based on the rank of the currently placed crafting facility</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Higher ranked crafting facilities include all lower-level recipes.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Hidden recipes will become available when special materials are acquired.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">There is a hidden recipe that requires different materials each expedition.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Items that are available to be crafted can be checked at any time by viewing the Craft UI.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Crafting items other than the basic items is only possible while in the vicinity of a Crafting Facility.</span></li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Alchemy</span>
<ul>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Craft potions that provide benefits such as stat enhancement and health recovery.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Potions that can be crafted can be checked at any time by viewing the Craft UI.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Alchemy is only possible while in the vicinity of the Alchemy Pot.</span></li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Cooking</span>
<ul>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Create Food dishes by combining up to 3 food items.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">The amount of available cooking slots depends on the rank of the cooking facility being used.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Cooking recipes are not provided, you'll need to experiment and discover what it takes to create each dish!</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">A cooking facility is required to cook food and cooking is only possible while near the facility.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Remember to move the cooked food back to your inventory!</span></li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>Crafting UI</strong></span></p>
<ul>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Inventory</span>
<ul>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">The inventory button can be opened by clicking the icon at the bottom of the mini map or using the inventory button/hotkey. (Note: General inventory cannot be opened while in an expedition)</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Acquired items are automatically moved to the inventory.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">20 slots are provided by default, additional slots can be obtained by crafting item bags.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">The Expiration timer/durability are displayed in the item icon.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">The slot background changes based on the item type to help differentiate item types.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Sort and Consolidate buttons are provided at the bottom of the UI to help manage obtained items.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">The Craft button at the bottom opens the craft UI when selected.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Items that can be used in the inventory can be placed on hotkeys for easier use.</span></li>
<li>Items expiring the soonest will be used first.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Crafting UI</span>
<ul>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Can be opened by selecting the [Craft] button at the bottom of the mini map, the [Craft] button in the inventory, or by clicking a crafting facility.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Provides a list of recipes for items that can be crafted.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Recipes are added by upgrading crafting facilities or collecting hidden materials.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Found recipes are maintained until the end of the expedition.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">When you select an item to be crafted, the item name/description/required material is displayed on the right side. (Note: you will need to mouse over the item in the ui to see the entire description)</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">The current number of items in possession and number of required items is displayed on the material icon.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">You can see Item tooltips by hovering the mouse over the material icon.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Crafting, other than the basic items, can only be done while near a crafting facility.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Crafted items are automatically moved to the inventory. If the inventory is full and a previously crafted bag has empty space, it will be moved to the bag.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Certain buildings can be upgraded by crafting the higher ranked version of the facility.</span></li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Cooking UI</span>
<ul>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">The UI can be opened by clicking the nearest cooking facility.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">The number of slots for ingredients varies depending on the type of cooking building.</span>
<ul>
<li><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>Bonfire</strong>: 1 slot - Only simple grilled dishes are possible. Low efficiency.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>Campfire for cooking</strong>: 2 slots - Can cook by combining 1 or 2 ingredients.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>Countertop</strong>: 3 slots - Can cook by combining 1 or 3 ingredients.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">1 slot per 1 material of any type. If you want to put 2 of the same item, they will occupy 2 slots.</span></li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Drag and drop or double-click inventory items to add them to the cooking slot then click the [Cook] button.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">If you want to remove an item from the cooking UI, drag and drop it into your inventory or right-click the item.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">When you click the [Cook] button, it is completed immediately, and the finished cooking item is displayed.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">If you double-click or right click a cooked item while it's in the cooking UI, it will be moved to the inventory.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">If you cook an invalid combination of food, a Mystery Dish is created.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">The cooked item will remain available in the cooking facility if the UI is accidently closed</span></li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>Crafting Materials</strong></span></p>
<ul>
<li>All resources required for making essential starting items such as worktables, Stone Axes, etc. are obtainable through harvesting.</li>
<li>Lava Stone, a rare crafting resource, can be obtained through mining and defeating difficult monsters, Goblin Leaders, and the Goblin King.</li>
<li>Harvested resources are mainly used to craft items for survival and relic excavation.</li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Monster resources are mainly used for crafting items and potions useful for defeating high level monsters and bosses.</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>Facility</strong></span></p>
<ul>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Structures that can be installed on the map directly and interacted with.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Has HP and can be destroyed by monster attacks.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">When double-clicking an item, the structure is placed at the current character's location in the direction the character is looking.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Cannot be placed if the area is smaller than the structure.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Cannot be placed in an underwater area.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Buildings are not damaged by players attacks.</span></li>
<li>If a building is no longer needed or if you want to install another building in that location, it can be destroyed by crafting and using the Demolition Hammer.</li>
<li>Some of the materials used to craft structures will drop when they're destroyed.</li>
</ul>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>Equip</strong></span></p>
<ul>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Character appearance changes when equipping equipment.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Some actions require special equipment (Ex: you need Pickax to gather stones).</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Provide bonus stats (Speed, Defense, HP, etc.) and exclusive defense effects (atmospheric effect penalty reduction, etc.)</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Some advanced equipment provides powerful skills.</span>
<ul>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Characters do not have any skills.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">A way to obtain relevant skills while equipping equipment items.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Skill loss when unequipping equipment item.</span></li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Each equipment has durability, and when all durability is consumed, the equipment is destroyed.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #000000;">Matches and torches can be used as a light source for a duration.</span></li>
</ul>

<h3><span style="color: #800080;"><strong><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><a name="Amazing"></a>[DJ Selena's Showtime!] Amazing Ignition</span></span></strong></span></h3>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>UTC: December 14th (after maintenance) – January 10th at 11:59 PM</strong></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><strong>Requirement: Lv. 101 and above characters (Zero characters must have completed up to Chapter 2) that have completed the "[Ignition Flame Festival] World Best! Mister Ignition!" quest.</strong></span></p>
<ul>
<li>Complete the "[DJ Selena's Showtime!] Amazing Ignition Finale" quest from DJ Selena or the event notifier from the left side of the screen to obtain the "DJ Selena's Showtime! - Amazing Ignition Finale" skill.</li>
<li>The "DJ Selena's Showtime! - Amazing Ignition Finale" skill automatically activates when you enter a hunting zone with enemies near your level (20 levels below and 20 levels above).</li>
<li>The "DJ Selena's Showtime! - Amazing Ignition Finale" activates only in maps with enemies near your level and doesn't activate in maps with the following conditions:
<ul>
<li>Elite Boss is summoned</li>
<li>Another player's fever skill is activated</li>
<li>Event maps</li>
<li>Special maps (Mirror World, story quest maps, etc.)</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>When you defeat enemies near your level while "DJ Selena's Showtime! - Amazing Ignition Finale" skill is active, the Fever Gauge will fill up one slot for every 50 enemies defeated.</li>
<li>One skill, either the normal Fever Skill or lucky Fever Skill, will activate for every 250 enemies near your level defeated.
<ul>
<li>Enemies near your level defeated with the Fever Skill do not count towards the 250 enemies near your level required to activate "DJ Selena's Showtime! - Amazing Ignition."</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Mister Ignition's attack count and range differ based on the activated Fever Skill.
<ul>
<li><strong>[Amazing Ignition: This Is Nothing]:</strong> Mister Ignition appears to attack nearby enemies 5 times and you obtain EXP when he disappears.</li>
<li><strong>[Amazing Ignition: Amazing Movement]:</strong> Mister Ignition appears to attack nearby enemies in a wider range for 5 seconds and you can obtain even more EXP when he disappears.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>The skill is disabled when you reach the daily fever activations (20 per character)</li>
<li>The summoned Mister Ignition is visible only to you. It receives the EXP increase effect and death penalty EXP reduction effect.</li>
<li>The "DJ Selena's Showtime! - Flame Group Dance" skill will disappear on <strong>December 14th at 12:00 AM UTC</strong>.</li>
</ul>
